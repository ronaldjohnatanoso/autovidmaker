Speaker 1: 
There’s a version of me still sitting at that playground. Bare feet on warm gravel. Waiting for someone who never showed up. I think about him sometimes. I think he still believes in everything.
We wear every age we’ve ever been. The child. The teenager. The version who thought they had it all figured out. They don’t vanish. They whisper. Sometimes, they scream.
